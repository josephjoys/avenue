Destinations

Choose your destination and book now to get the best deals.

Domestic

Delhi

India’s capital territory. Popular attractions includes Red Fort, Qutb Minar, India gate etc.

Andaman Islands

Roughly 300 islands are known for their palm-lined, white-sand beaches, mangroves and tropical rainforests.

Jammu & Kashmir

Located in Himalayan mountains. Srinagar, Ladak, Raghunath Temple, Chemerey monastery are the main attractions

Tamil Nadu

A South Indian state, famed for its Dravidian-style Hindu temples. Madurai Meenakshi Amman temple, Kanyakumari, Rameshwaram, Tanjavoor and Ooty are popular places in Tamil Nadu.

Sikkim

Part of the Himalayas, the area has a dramatic landscape that includes India’s highest mountain, 8,586m Kangchenjunga. Sikkim is also home to glaciers, alpine meadows and thousands of varieties of wildflowers

Kerala

Kerala is known for its palm-lined beaches and backwaters, a network of canals. Inland are the Western Ghats, mountains whose slopes support tea, coffee and spice plantations as well as wildlife

International

Thailand

Thailand is known for tropical beaches, opulent royal palaces, ancient ruins and ornate temples displaying figures of Buddha

Malaysia

Malaysia is a Southeast Asian and it is popular for its beaches, rainforests and mix of Malay, Chinese, Indian and European cultural influences

Singapore

Singapore is a global financial center with a tropical climate and multicultural population. Its known for temples and Beach.
